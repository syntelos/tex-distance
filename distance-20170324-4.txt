Disbelief and denial exist, today, as infidelity to the mind-body identity, unity, the breath of life.  This infidelity manifests through the suspicious rationality that would substitute objectivity for subjectivity, science for art, ideology for thought, conformity for creativity.  The rationalization of identity betrays the self, the breath of life, the will to dispose of the gift of life according to one’s thoughts and senses and ambitions.
